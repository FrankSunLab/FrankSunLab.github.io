Operational Ratios by Company

Date: October 1, 2024  18:32:39

Disclaimer:
The report is prepared based on public financial reports and the following formulas, without considering any specific adjustments:

Formulas Used:

Days of Collection:
$$
\text{Days of Collection} = \left( \frac{\text{Accounts Receivable}}{\text{Revenue}} \right) \times 365
$$

Days of Inventory:
$$
\text{Days of Inventory} = \left( \frac{\text{Inventory}}{\text{Cost of Goods Sold}} \right) \times 365
$$

Days of Procurement:
$$
\text{Days of Procurement} = \left( \frac{\text{Accounts Payable}}{\text{Cost of Goods Sold}} \right) \times 365
$$
